More About Ke Huang

Dr Ke Huang MBBS(Syd) FRACS(Orth) FAOrthA, orthopaedic surgeon, graduated from Sydney University, successfully completing his orthopaedic training in Sydney in 2005. He is currently a VMO and Clinical Lecturer for Sydney University at Nepean Public Hospital. He is an International Affiliated Member of the American Academy of Orthopaedic surgeons and a surgical educator for the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons. He is a general orthopaedic surgeon with a special interest in upper and lower limb arthroscopy and arthroplasty. He fluent in Mandarin Chinese.
