Directions and parking

Located near the corner of Waverley Road and Tennyson Street, it takes about 25 minutes driving or train ride (Caufiled Train Station) from the city. There is street parking available and we are easily accessible by the trams that run down Waverley Road. We would be well located for anyone in the areas of Malvern East, Malvern, Carnegie, Caulfield, Murrumbeena, Glen Iris, Armadale, Camberwell, Chadstone and Bentleigh.
